# Electronics-Sales-Dashboard
📌 Project Overview
The main objective of this dashboard is to analyze electronics sales data and present key business insights through interactive and visually appealing reports.
It helps businesses and stakeholders quickly understand:
-Sales trends 📈
-Profit performance 💰
-Customer purchasing behavior 👥
-Product category performance 📦

📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard highlights the following key metrics:
💰 Total Sales: 256M
📈 Total Profit: 51M
📦 Quantity Sold: 6067
These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

📉 Dashboard Insights
👥 Customer Type Analysis
Shows the distribution between New Customers and Returning Customers.
Helps analyze customer retention and acquisition.

📅 Monthly Sales Trend
A line chart that displays sales performance across different months.
Helps identify seasonal trends and peak sales periods.

📦 Sales by Product Category
This visualization shows revenue generated from categories such as:
📺 TV
📱 Mobile
💻 Laptop
🎧 Headphones
⌚ Smartwatch
📟 Tablet

🗺️ Sales and Profit by State
A map visualization showing sales and profit across different states in India.
Helps identify high-performing regions.

💳 Orders by Payment Mode
Displays the most commonly used payment methods:
💳 Credit Card
🏦 Debit Card
💵 Cash
📲 UPI
📅 EMI

📈 Monthly Profit Trend
Tracks profit changes throughout the year, helping identify high-profit months.
🏷️ Sales by Brand
Compares sales performance of brands like:
Samsung
Apple
Sony
Lenovo
JBL
LG
